Special Dragon Abilities
Hybrid Dragons: Apply both elemental breath effects when breath result is rolled
Ivory Hybrids: Apply elemental breath effect, affected by spells of their element or ivory
White Dragons: Double damage from claws/jaws/tail/wing, double treasure results
Drake Form (12 faces)
Jaws
A dragon's jaws inflict twelve points of damage on an army. Also counts as the 'ID' for ID based SAIs that effect Dragons.
Dragon Breath
Against another dragon, dragon breath inflicts five (ten for a White Dragon) points of damage; roll the dragon again and apply the new result as well.
* Against armies, five health-worth of units in the target army are killed.
* In addition, an effect based on the color affects the army:
** BLACK (Dragon Plague): The army ignores all of its ID results until the beginning of its next turn.
** BLUE (Lightning Bolt): The army's melee results are halved until the beginning of its next turn. Results are rounded down.
** YELLOW (Petrify): The army's maneuver results are halved until the beginning of its next turn. Results are rounded down.
** GREEN (Poisonous Cloud): The army's missile results are halved until the beginning of its next turn. Results are rounded down.
** RED (Dragon Fire): Roll the units killed by this dragon's breath attack. Those that do not generate a save result are buried.
** IVORY (Life Drain): No additional effect.
** WHITE (Terrain Empathy): An additional five health-worth of units in the army are killed. The army is affected by a breath of both colors of the terrain.

Wing Left
A dragon's wings inflict five points of damage on an army. After the attack, if the dragon is still alive, it flies away. It returns to it's summoning pool.
Wing Right
A dragon's wings inflict five points of damage on an army. After the attack, if the dragon is still alive, it flies away. It returns to it's summoning pool.
Belly Front
The dragon's five automatic saves do not count during this attack. In other words, five points of damage will slay the dragon this turn.
Belly Rear
The dragon's five automatic saves do not count during this attack. In other words, five points of damage will slay the dragon this turn.

Dragon Summoning Rules
Elemental Dragons: Can be summoned from terrain or summoning pool using matching element magic
Hybrid Dragons: Can be summoned from terrain using either element's magic
Ivory Dragons: Can only be summoned from summoning pool using any single element magic
Ivory Hybrids: Can be summoned from terrain using their element's magic or ivory effects
White Dragons: Can only be summoned using "Summon White Dragon" spell (14 cost, any element combination)
Force Values: Most dragons count as 1 force, White Dragons count as 2 force when assembling armies
